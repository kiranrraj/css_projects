Starters

Falafel
Falafel is a deep-fried ball made from ground chickpeas or fava beans. The ultimate in fast food, it is commonly served in pita bread or wrapped in a flatbread.
Chicken soup
Chicken soup consists of a clear chicken broth, often with pieces of chicken or vegetables; common additions are pasta, noodles, dumplings, or grains such as rice and barley.
Onion rings
An onion ring is a form of appetizer or side dish in American cuisine. They consist of a cross-sectional "ring" of onion dipped in batter or bread crumbs and then deep fried.
Caesar Salad
A Caesar salad is made of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice, olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper
French fries
French fries, or simply fries, chips, finger chips, hot chips or French-fried potatoes, are batonnet or allumette-cut deep-fried potatoes
Chicken fingers/ nuggets
Chicken fingers, also known as chicken tenders, chicken strips or chicken fillets, are chicken meat prepared from the pectoralis minor muscles of the animal.
Hummus
Hummus is made with garbanzo beans with a quarter cup of tahini, the juice from lemon, two tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil, garlic cloves, and salt to taste

Luncheon Menu

Biriyani Vegetable / Chicken / Lamb
Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating from India. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and/or vegetables.
Fried chicken
Fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried.
Spaghetti
Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine. Like other pasta, spaghetti is made of milled wheat and water
Bangers and Mash
A traditional dish from the British Isles, this delicious meal of sausages and mashed potatoes – topped with onion gravy
Hamburger
Hamburger consists of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside the sliced bread/bun. This is either fried, grilled or smoked and served with lettuce,cheese, tomato and pickles
Lasagna
The ingredients itself sound mouthwatering - meats, pasta, vegetables, tomato sauce, and lots and lots of cheese.
Mac & cheese
Macaroni and cheese—also called mac 'n' cheese in the United States, is a dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar.

Dinner Menu

Fried chicken
Fried chicken, as the name suggests, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces deeply fried, giving it a crisp coating. The chicken is then seasoned with herbs and spices
Quesadilla
Quesadilla is a Mexican fast food snack consists of grilled folded tortillas stuffed with vegetables, meat or poultry, and of course – cheese.
Paneer Butter Masala
Paneer is a type of fresh cheese, and when paired with butter masala, it is the perfect combination of creaminess and spiciness. This dish is served with rice or tandoori roti.
Sushi
Prepared with vinegared rice,seafood, vegetables, and sometimes fruits. Sushi is always accompanied by pickled ginger, a fiery horseradish and soy sauce.
Ramen
Ramen is a Japanese dish made of wheat noodles and served in the broth along with vegetables and meat
Kebab
Kebab consist of ground meat or seafood, fruits, and vegetables in some cases and are cooked on a skewer with a big fire underneath
Goulash
Goulash is prepared from either beef, pork, veal, or lamb. The meat is cut into pieces and neatly seasoned with salt. It is then browned with oil and sliced onion.

Dessert

Banana split
A banana split is an American ice cream-based dessert. There are many variations, but the classic banana split is made with three scoops of ice cream served between the split banana
Molten lava cakes
Molten chocolate cake is a popular dessert that combines the elements of a chocolate cake and a soufflé
Cheesecake
Cheesecake is a sweet dessert. The main layer consists of a mixture of fresh cheese, eggs and sugar. Bottom layer consists of base made from crushed cookies, graham crackers.
Shortcake
Shortcake is a sweet cake or biscuit, leavened with baking powder or baking soda. They are generally less dense and more crunchy and dry than shortbread.
Apple Cobbler
Apple crisp is a dessert made with a streusel topping. An apple crumble is a dessert of baked chopped apples topped with rolled oats and brown sugar.
Cinnamon rolls
A cinnamon roll consists of a rolled sheet of yeast-leavened dough onto which a cinnamon and sugar is sprinkled over a thin coat of butter and baked or deep fried.
Apple pie
An apple pie is a pie in which the principal filling ingredient is apple. It is often served with whipped cream, ice cream, or cheddar cheese. It is generally double-crusted, with pastry.
