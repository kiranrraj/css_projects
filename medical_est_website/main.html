About

We Behzad Medical Est. is one of the oldest drugs and medical equipment distribution company in Kingdom of Bahrain,established in 1947 by Late Dr. Qassim Mohamed Behzad. We are working with various partners in new way to improve the availability of most modern medical equipments and drugs to the Bahrain healthcare sector. We recognize that with most modern technology our healthcare system will be more safe, effective ande useful.

We deals Drugs Products, Lab Products, Cosmetic Products, Hospital Furniture , Medical Equipment, X-ray/Radio Active Products, Surgical Consumables/ Instruments, Physiotherapy Products etc. Some of the companies we deal with are Medtronics, Welch Allyn, Interacoustics, Smiths medicals, EZEm, coloplast, APEX, Abena, Spimaco, Tabuk, Leo, PSI etc.

Distribution Channels

  • Direct Sales by Sales Representatives
  • Awarded through Tender participation.
  • 7 Outlets (6 Pharmacies & 1 Showroom)

Promotion Channels

  • Through Medical / Sales representatives
  • Through Biomedical Engineering Team
  • Through seminars, exhibition & fairs

Mission

To contribute to Bahrain's human welfare by introducing most modern products,equipments and drugs that can alleviate pain and provide faster recovery. To provide high quality products at a fair price which will reduce the overall treatment cost.
