Our Specials

Crispy Fried Chicken

Crispy fried chicken is a standard dish in the Cantonese cuisine of southern China and Hong Kong. The chicken is fried in such a way that the skin is extremely crunchy, but the white meat is relatively soft.

Beef Lasagne

Traditional beef lasagne recipe made from scratch including a slow cooked bolognese sauce, and cheesy bechamel sauce.

Texas Sheet Cake

If you love chocolate, and we mean really truly love, then classic Texas sheet cake is exactly what your sweet tooth craves. Between the scratch-made cake and the decadent frosting, this dessert is a chocolate lover’s dream come true.
