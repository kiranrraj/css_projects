About Us

Who we are

We are Hell Rocks, only our name is modern, we prepare traditional kerala cuisine to satisfy your taste buds. We started in 2010, with all the experience we got from previous years, we are at our best- in hospitality, service and preprationing tasty food. Serve quality food made from quality ingredients is our motto. Kerala cuisine is a culinary style originated in the Kerala, a region in the south of India. Kerala cuisine offers a multitude of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes prepared using fish, poultry and red meat with rice a typical accompaniment. Chillies, curry leaves, coconut, mustard seeds, turmeric, tamarind, asafoetida and other spices are also used in the preparation.

Built on the principle that food tastes better when shared.
